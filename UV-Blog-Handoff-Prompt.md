### GLOBAL BLOG SETTINGS

**Blog URL structure:** unlimitedville.com/blog/[slug]
**Author name:** Unlimitedville
**Default category options:** Guides, Comparisons, Tips
**Article schema on every post:** headline, author, datePublished, dateModified, publisher (Unlimitedville), image

**On-page SEO checklist (apply to each article):**
- Title tag under 60 characters with primary keyword
- Meta description 150-160 characters
- Exactly one H1 per page (the article title)
- H2s and H3s use secondary keywords where natural
- Primary keyword appears in the first 100 words
- At least 2 internal links per article (to other blog posts + service pages)
- At least 1 external link to an authoritative source per article
- All images have descriptive alt text with relevant keywords
- Article schema markup applied
- Open Graph tags for social sharing (og:title, og:description, og:image)

**Navigation:** Add a "Blog" link to the main site navigation if one doesn't exist yet.

**Blog index page:** Create a /blog page that lists all articles with title, excerpt (first 150 characters of the article), featured image, and publish date. Most recent first.
