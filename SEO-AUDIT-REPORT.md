# Unlimitedville SEO Audit Report
**Date:** April 1, 2026
**Domain:** unlimitedville.com
**Audit Type:** Full Site Audit + Keyword Deep Dive

---

## Executive Summary

The Unlimitedville site has a strong technical SEO foundation after this optimization pass. All pages now have unique, keyword-rich title tags and meta descriptions, dynamic structured data (FAQ, Product, Article, Organization, BreadcrumbList), and proper canonical/OG tag management. The biggest remaining opportunities are in content gap coverage -- there are dozens of high-intent keyword variations the site doesn't currently have dedicated pages for, and competitors like Nomad Internet, OTR Mobile, and TravlFi are actively creating comparison and educational content to capture this traffic. The top 3 priorities: (1) build out dedicated landing pages for high-value keyword clusters, (2) publish the blog content strategy to capture informational queries, and (3) create head-to-head comparison pages against each named competitor.

### Critical Architecture Note

The site currently uses hash-based routing (#/plans, #/rural, etc.). Search engine crawlers may not index hash routes reliably. For maximum SEO impact, the highest-leverage technical investment is migrating to either server-side rendering (Next.js, etc.) or a prerendering solution that serves clean URLs (/plans, /rural) with full HTML to crawlers. This is the single biggest SEO bottleneck for the site.
