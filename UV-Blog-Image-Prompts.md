**Style direction for all images:** Clean, modern, slightly editorial. Warm color palette leaning toward blues and greens (nature + tech). Avoid stock photo clichés (no smiling person pointing at a router). Think more editorial illustration or cinematic photography style. No text or words in the images.

## IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

For all images:
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 for hero images, 3:2 for in-article images
- **Minimum resolution:** 1200px wide (2400px preferred for retina)
- **Format:** PNG or WebP preferred, JPEG acceptable
- **File size target:** Under 500KB after compression (use TinyPNG or similar)
- **No text, watermarks, or brand logos in any image**
- **No AI artifacts (extra fingers, warped text, etc.) -- regenerate if needed**

Total images needed: 11
- Article 1: 3 images (1 hero + 2 in-article)
- Article 2: 3 images (1 hero + 2 in-article)
- Article 3: 3 images (1 hero + 2 in-article)
- Article 4: 4 images (1 hero + 3 in-article)
